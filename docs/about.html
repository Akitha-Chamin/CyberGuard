About The Platform

The Mission


Cybercrime is escalating at an alarming rate. Traditional reporting methods are often fragmented and slow. CyberGuard was developed to provide a centralized, secure environment for individuals and organizations to report suspicious activities.


Our goal is to transform individual reports into actionable intelligence, allowing for faster threat detection and response.
